# PennyExplained.com - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Mission:** Making personal finance simple and accessible for beginners.

**Voice:** Clear, friendly, educational, non-judgmental

**Tagline Options:**
- "Personal Finance Made Simple"
- "Your Money, Explained"
- "Finance Basics, Clearly Explained"

---

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- **Primary Logo:** Full logo with penny icon + text (logo.svg)
- **Icon Only:** For social media, app icons (favicon.svg)
- **Minimum Size:** 120px width for readability
- **Clear Space:** Maintain 15px padding around logo

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- **Charcoal:** #1f2937 (rgb 31, 41, 55)
  - Use for: Main text, headings, primary UI elements
  
- **Warm Amber:** #f59e0b (rgb 245, 158, 11)
  - Use for: Accents, CTAs, highlights, links

**Neutral Colors:**
- **White:** #ffffff
- **Light Gray:** #f8fafc (backgrounds)
- **Medium Gray:** #64748b (secondary text)
- **Border Gray:** #e2e8f0

**Semantic Colors:**
- **Success Green:** #10b981 (positive results, savings)
- **Warning Orange:** #f97316 (caution, important notes)
- **Info Blue:** #3b82f6 (tips, informational callouts)

### Typography

**Headings:**
```
Font Family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', 'Roboto', 'Oxygen', 'Ubuntu', sans-serif
Font Weight: 600 (Semi-bold) to 700 (Bold)
```

**Body Text:**
```
Font Family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', 'Roboto', sans-serif
Font Weight: 400 (Regular)
Font Size: 16px (base), 18px (articles for readability)
Line Height: 1.6 to 1.8
Color: #1f2937
```

**Font Scale:**
- H1: 2.5rem (40px)
- H2: 2rem (32px)
- H3: 1.5rem (24px)
- H4: 1.25rem (20px)
- Body: 1rem (16px)
- Small: 0.875rem (14px)

### Spacing System

Use 8px base unit:
- xs: 4px
- sm: 8px
- md: 16px
- lg: 24px
- xl: 32px
- 2xl: 48px
- 3xl: 64px

### Design Principles

1. **Whitespace is King:** Generous padding and margins
2. **Hierarchy:** Clear visual distinction between elements
3. **Accessibility:** WCAG AA contrast ratios minimum
4. **Mobile-First:** Touch-friendly targets (44px minimum)
5. **Readability:** Max content width 750px, ample line-height

## Content Style

### Article Structure
1. Clear H1 title
2. Brief introduction (2-3 sentences)
3. Table of contents (for articles >800 words)
4. Sectioned content with H2/H3
5. Practical examples with real numbers
6. Summary/key takeaways
7. Related articles

### Writing Style
- Use "you" (second person)
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Bold for emphasis (sparingly)
- Define jargon in plain language
- Real-world examples with realistic numbers

### Example Tone

❌ **Don't:** "Maximize your ROI by leveraging compound interest vehicles."
✅ **Do:** "When you save money regularly, it grows over time. Here's how it works."

---

## Ad Placement Guidelines

**Above the Fold:**
- 1 leaderboard (728x90) or responsive unit
- Below hero/main heading

**In-Content:**
- After first 2-3 paragraphs
- Mid-article (around 40-50% scroll)
- Between major sections (H2 breaks)

**Sidebar:**
- Sticky sidebar ad unit (desktop only)
- 300x250 or 300x600

**End of Article:**
- 1 unit before related articles

**Spacing:** Minimum 16px padding around all ad units

## Brand Do's and Don'ts

✅ **Do:**
- Keep it simple and scannable
- Use real examples with actual dollar amounts
- Maintain friendly, encouraging tone
- Prioritize mobile experience
- Use plenty of whitespace

❌ **Don't:**
- Use financial jargon without explanation
- Make promises about financial outcomes
- Overwhelm with ads (balance UX and revenue)
- Use stock photos of people (keep illustrations/icons)
- Use all caps in body text
